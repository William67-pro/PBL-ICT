Why we do this website

We created this website to spread awareness about pollution and help people understand how harmful it is to our environment, animals, and communities. Many people use plastic every day without realizing the long-term effects it can have on the planet. Through this website, we want to educate people about the causes of plastic pollution, the damage it creates in oceans, rivers, cities, and natural habitats, and the importance of reducing plastic waste in our daily lives.
