📘 What is Pollution?

Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances into the environment. These pollutants damage the air, water, and land, affecting human health and wildlife. Pollution is mostly caused by human activities such as transportation, industries, and waste disposal.

🌫 Types of Pollution

Air Pollution

Harmful gases released into the air from vehicles and factories.

Water Pollution

Waste dumped into rivers and oceans affecting marine life.

Land Pollution

Garbage and plastic that damage soil and environment.

Plastic Pollution

Garbage and plastic that damage soil and environment.
