Introduction

Pollution is one of the most serious global challenges today. It affects human health, ecosystems, and the climate. From harmful gases in the air to plastic waste in oceans, pollution threatens the future of our planet.

Main Pollutants

  • Air Pollution – the contamination of the atmosphere by harmful gases
  • Water Pollution – the contamination of water bodies (such as oceans, lakes, rivers, and groundwater) by harmful substances
  • Soil Pollution or Land Pollution – the degradation of the Earth's land surfaces caused by the accumulation of solid waste
  • Plastic Pollution – the accumulation of synthetic plastic waste in the environment
