How Can We Help

Way to reduce air pollution

  • Solar energy instead of fossil fuels
  • Plant trees in cities
  • Use energy-efficient devices
  • Use public transport / electric vehicles

Way to reduce water pollution

  • Rainwater harvesting
  • Water quality sensors (detect pollution early)
  • Don’t dump waste into rivers/drains
  • Use eco-friendly detergents

Way to reduce land or soil pollution

  • Composting food waste → turns into fertilizer
  • Use less packaging
  • Organic farming (less chemicals)
  • Plant more trees

Way to reduce plastic pollution

  • Use reusable bags, bottles, containers
  • Recycling systems with sorting machines
  • Machines that collect plastic from oceans
  • Use biodegradable plastics
